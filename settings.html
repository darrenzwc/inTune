Spectrum Frequency Processor
A visual representation of the spectrum of frequencies in a sound or other signal as they vary with time. It displays frequency on the vertical axis, time on the horizontal axis, and signal intensity through colors or shades, enabling detailed analysis of the audio's spectral components. The program processes the audio and identifies the main beat (known as main melody or speaker" as best as possible identifying it through a reddish-orange hue.
